supa: tolerate not-yet-migrated columns so persistence never breaks

Adding retell_id to the qa_sessions insert would make PostgREST 400
(PGRST204) until the column is created, dropping the entire session row.
_post now detects "Could not find the 'X' column", strips X from the
rows, and retries — so a new field is forward-compatible with a pending
ALTER TABLE and never blocks persistence of the other columns.
